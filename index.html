About Me

I’m Milthon Efrain Sigcha, a Graphic Design Engineer from Ecuador and a BYU–Idaho student. For several years, I’ve been creating visual content, corporate branding, and graphic design, helping businesses build strong and memorable identities. My design style is minimalist, vibrant, and elegant, always focused on capturing the unique essence of each project. I’m currently in training as a web developer, aiming to combine my creative background with digital tools that enhance my designs. I’m passionate about learning, innovating, and continuing to grow professionally in the world of design and technology.
